Your TMNT dudes!

Leonardo

The disciplined and courageous leader of the Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles. He wields two katanas and is known for his strong sense of responsibility and determination to protect his brothers and the city from evil.

Donatello

The brains of the TMNT team. He is a skilled inventor and technician, often using his knowledge to create gadgets and weapons to aid in their battles. Donatello wields a bo staff and is known for his problem-solving skills and analytical thinking.

Michelangelo

The fun-loving and free-spirited member of the Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles. He is known for his love of pizza, his nunchaku skills, and his upbeat personality. Michelangelo brings humor to the team and often lightens the mood during intense situations.

Raphael

The hot-headed and rebellious member of the TMNT. He wields twin sai and is known for his aggressive fighting style. Raphael often struggles with his temper but is fiercely loyal to his brothers and will do whatever it takes to protect them.

Splinter

Wise and fatherly figure in the Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles universe. As the turtles' sensei and mentor, he is a skilled martial artist and a master of ninjutsu. Splinter's calm demeanor and profound wisdom guide the turtles in their journey to become heroes, teaching them valuable life lessons along the way.

April O'Neil

Fearless and resourceful human ally of the Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles. As a skilled journalist, she often finds herself in the midst of danger, but her determination and intelligence help her uncover the truth. April serves as a crucial link between the turtles and the human world, providing them with information and support.
